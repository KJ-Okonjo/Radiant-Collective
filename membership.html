Membership

Membership at Radiant Collective Capital is by application only. We bring together a curated community of investors, operators, and thought leaders committed to driving capital towards high-quality African opportunities.

Who Should Apply

Our membership is designed for:

  • High-net-worth African women investors
  • Family offices seeking African deal flow
  • Institutional investors focused on emerging markets
  • Experienced operators building businesses across Africa

What's Included

Members gain access to:

  • Exclusive Deal Flow: Vetted investment opportunities across sectors and geographies
  • Community Network: Direct access to co-investors, advisors, and operators
  • Market Intelligence: Reports, insights, and analysis on African markets
  • Events & Convenings: Quarterly gatherings, virtual sessions, and curated introductions

Application Process

Membership applications are reviewed on a rolling basis. To apply, please create an account and complete the membership application form. Our team will review your submission and reach out within 10 business days.

For questions about membership, please contact us.
