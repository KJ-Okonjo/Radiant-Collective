About Us

Radiant Collective Capital is a membership-based investment platform built by African women, for African women. We connect high-net-worth individuals and institutional investors with vetted, high-quality opportunities across Africa and the diaspora.

Our Mission

We exist to bridge the capital gap for African women entrepreneurs and investors. By curating exclusive deal flow and building trusted operator networks, we ensure our members gain access to opportunities that would otherwise remain invisible.

What We Do

Through rigorous vetting, strategic curation, and community-driven due diligence, we unlock investment opportunities with precision and trust. Our members benefit from:

  • Exclusive access to pre-vetted deals
  • A network of trusted operators and co-investors
  • Deep market intelligence across key African markets
  • Community-driven insights and collaborative due diligence

Who We Serve

Our members include sophisticated investors, family offices, and institutional players committed to supporting African innovation and growth. If you're an African woman investor seeking deal flow, or an operator building across Africa, explore membership.

Leadership

Radiant Collective Capital is led by a team of experienced investors and operators with deep roots across African markets. Learn more about our team and advisory network in our contact section.
