Our Purpose

We exist to close the wealth gap, demystify investment, and equip women across Africa and the diaspora to rise as owners, decision-makers, and legacy-builders.

Our Why

Despite driving economies, building businesses, and leading families, African women are still locked out of the structures that create generational wealth.

We don't own the banks.

We don't lead the deals.

Radiant changes that.

We're creating a high-trust, high-access ecosystem where women invest from the ground up, together.
